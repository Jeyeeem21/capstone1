INCORRECT TERMINOLOGY:
  ❌ "Staff" - too generic

CORRECT TERMINOLOGY:
  ✅ "Secretary" - the role that handles POS/orders

USER ROLES IN SYSTEM:
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Super Admin - Full system access
2. Admin - Management access
3. Secretary - POS/Order handling (NOT "Staff")
4. Driver - Delivery only
5. Customer - Customer portal

SECRETARY ROLE CLARIFICATION:
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Secretary is the role that:
  ✅ Handles Point of Sale (POS)
  ✅ Creates orders
  ✅ Records payments
  ✅ Manages customer transactions
  ✅ Does NOT choose payment methods (customer chooses!)

IMPORTANT: Secretary does NOT select payment method!
  - Customer tells secretary how they want to pay
  - Secretary only records what customer chose
  - Secretary can only choose: Staggered or Not Staggered

╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                    PAYMENT METHOD ACCESS BY ROLE                         ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

SECRETARY (POS):
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
What Secretary CAN choose:
  ✅ Staggered Payment: Yes or No (checkbox)
  ✅ If staggered: Setup payment schedule

What Secretary CANNOT choose:
  ❌ Payment Method (Cash, GCash, PDO, Credit)
  ❌ Customer decides payment method!

Secretary's Role:
  1. Customer says: "I'll pay via GCash"
  2. Secretary records: Payment Method = GCash
  3. Secretary asks: "Full payment or installments?"
  4. Customer says: "Installments"
  5. Secretary enables: ☑ Staggered Payment
  6. Secretary and customer setup schedule together

ADMIN/SUPER ADMIN:
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
What Admin CAN do:
  ✅ View all payment methods
  ✅ Approve/reject staggered payments
  ✅ Verify GCash payments
  ✅ Approve PDO payments
  ✅ Modify payment schedules
  ✅ Record payments for existing orders

CUSTOMER (Portal):
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
What Customer CAN choose:
  ✅ Payment Method (Cash/GCash/PDO/Credit)
  ✅ Staggered Payment: Yes or No
  ✅ Setup their own payment schedule
  ✅ Submit for approval (if needed)
