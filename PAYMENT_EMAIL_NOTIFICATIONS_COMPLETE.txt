OVERVIEW:
=========
Comprehensive email notification system for all payment-related actions.
Superadmin receives notifications for ALL payment changes.
Customers receive notifications when their payments are rejected.

This ensures transparency and keeps stakeholders informed about payment status changes.


EMAIL NOTIFICATIONS IMPLEMENTED:
================================

1. ✅ PAYMENT VERIFIED
   - Trigger: Admin verifies a payment (GCash, PDO, etc.)
   - Recipient: Super Admin
   - Email Class: PaymentVerifiedNotification.php
   - Template: emails/payment-verified.blade.php
   - Contains:
     * Payment ID & Order Number
     * Customer name
     * Amount
     * Payment method
     * Reference/Check number
     * Who verified it
     * Date verified

2. ✅ PAYMENT REJECTED/CANCELLED
   - Trigger: Admin rejects/cancels a payment
   - Recipients: Super Admin AND Customer
   - Email Class: PaymentRejectedNotification.php
   - Templates:
     * emails/payment-rejected-admin.blade.php (for superadmin)
     * emails/payment-rejected-customer.blade.php (for customer)
   - Contains:
     * Payment details
     * Rejection reason
     * Who rejected it
     * Customer-friendly explanation (for customer email)

3. ✅ PDO CHECK APPROVED
   - Trigger: Admin approves a PDO check (Stage 1 of two-stage approval)
   - Recipient: Super Admin
   - Email Class: PDOApprovedNotification.php
   - Template: emails/pdo-approved.blade.php
   - Contains:
     * Payment & Order details
     * Check number, bank, date
     * Who approved it
     * Status: "Awaiting Payment Clearance"

4. ✅ PAYMENT HELD FOR REVIEW
   - Trigger: Admin puts a payment on hold
   - Recipient: Super Admin
   - Email Class: PaymentHeldNotification.php
   - Template: emails/payment-held.blade.php
   - Contains:
     * Payment details
     * Reason for hold
     * Who placed it on hold
     * Requires further review action


BACKEND INTEGRATION:
===================

EmailService Methods Added (app/Services/EmailService.php):
-----------------------------------------------------------
1. notifyPaymentVerified($payment, $verifiedBy)
   - Sends payment verified notification to superadmin

2. notifyPaymentRejected($payment, $reason, $rejectedBy)
   - Sends rejection notification to BOTH superadmin and customer
   - Uses different templates for each recipient

3. notifyPDOApproved($payment, $approvedBy)
   - Sends PDO approval notification to superadmin

4. notifyPaymentHeld($payment, $reason, $heldBy)
   - Sends payment hold notification to superadmin


Controller Integration:
----------------------
✅ PaymentController.php
   - verify(): Sends notifyPaymentVerified()
   - hold(): Sends notifyPaymentHeld()
   - cancel(): Sends notifyPaymentRejected()
   - approvePDO(): Sends notifyPDOApproved()
   - rejectPDO(): Sends notifyPaymentRejected()

✅ StaggeredPaymentController.php
   - approvePDO(): Sends notifyPDOApproved() for installment PDOs

NOTIFICATION FLOW EXAMPLES:
===========================

Example 1: GCash Payment Verification
--------------------------------------
1. Customer submits GCash payment with proof
2. Admin reviews proof in Payment Transactions tab
3. Admin clicks "Verify" button
4. Backend:
   - Updates payment status to "verified"
   - Updates sale balances
   - Sends email to superadmin with payment details
5. Superadmin receives email: "Payment Verified - Order #ORD-xxx"
6. Customer doesn't receive email (already confirmed via order status)


Example 2: PDO Payment Rejection
---------------------------------
1. Customer submits PDO check
2. Admin reviews check in PDO Management
3. Admin clicks "Reject" with reason: "Invalid check number"
4. Backend:
   - Updates payment status to "cancelled"
   - Sends email to superadmin (alert format)
   - Sends email to customer (user-friendly format)
5. Superadmin receives: "Payment Rejected Alert - Order #ORD-xxx"
6. Customer receives: "Payment Rejected - Order #ORD-xxx"
   - Includes reason and instructions to contact business


Example 3: PDO Two-Stage Approval Flow
---------------------------------------
Stage 1: Check Approval
1. Admin approves PDO check
2. Backend:
   - Sets pdo_approval_status = "approved"
   - Sends email to superadmin: "PDO Check Approved"
3. Email reminds superadmin to mark as paid when check clears

Stage 2: Payment Clearance
1. Check clears at bank (days/weeks later)
2. Admin clicks "Mark as Paid" in PDO Management
3. Backend:
   - Updates status to "verified"
   - Sends email to superadmin: "Payment Verified"

TROUBLESHOOTING:
===============
If emails are not being sent:

1. Check Business Settings → Email Notifications
   - Ensure business_email is set
   - Ensure smtp_password is set (Gmail App Password)

2. Check Laravel Logs
   - File: laravel_backend/storage/logs/laravel.log
   - Look for: "Failed to send [type] email"

3. Check SendEmail Job Queue
   - Run: php artisan queue:work
   - Check if jobs are being processed

4. Test SMTP Connection
   - Send test email from Business Settings
   - Verify Gmail App Password is correct

5. Check Superadmin Email
   - Ensure superadmin account has valid email
   - Check spam/junk folder
