KEY DIFFERENCE:
  PDO = ONE future payment (single date, single amount)

QUESTION 2: "Sa pdo kapag ang isesend ko lang is down payment lang 
            staggered pa rin sya kasi magsesend pa rin naman sa sunod?"

ANSWER: ❌ NO, PDO with down payment is NOT staggered

Detailed Explanation:
  
  PDO with Down Payment:
    - Down payment: ₱10,000 (paid now)
    - PDO: ₱40,000 (ONE payment due May 15)
    - Total payments: 2 (down + PDO)
    - Structure: Simple (1 future payment)
  
  Staggered Payment:
    - Down payment: ₱10,000 (paid now)
    - Installment #1: ₱10,000 (due May 15)
    - Installment #2: ₱10,000 (due Jun 15)
    - Installment #3: ₱10,000 (due Jul 15)
    - Installment #4: ₱10,000 (due Aug 15)
    - Total payments: 5 (down + 4 installments)
    - Structure: Multiple scheduled payments
  
  Key Difference:
    PDO = "I'll pay the remaining ₱40,000 on ONE specific date"
    Staggered = "I'll pay ₱10,000 every month for 4 months"
  
  Both have down payment, but payment structure is different!
