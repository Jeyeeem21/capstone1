## Payment Methods Overview

### 1. Cash
- Immediate payment
- Enter amount tendered
- Calculate change
- No installment option

### 2. GCash
- Immediate payment
- Enter reference number
- Upload proof of payment
- No installment option

### 3. Credit ⭐ NEW
- Payment tracked as credit
- Can enable installment payment
- Customer info required
- Flexible payment schedule

### 4. Pay Later
- Payment pending
- Can enable installment payment
- Customer info required
- Flexible payment schedule

### 5. COD (Cash on Delivery)
- Payment upon delivery
- No installment option
- For delivery orders only

## When to Use Each Method

### Credit:
- Customer has credit terms with the business
- Regular customers with good payment history
- Large orders that need payment plans
- Business wants to track credit separately

### Pay Later:
- One-time deferred payment
- Customer will pay in full later
- Can also be split into installments
- More casual than credit terms

## Installment Payment Flow

### For Credit or Pay Later:

1. **Select Payment Method**
   - Choose "Credit" or "Pay Later"

2. **Enable Installments (Optional)**
   - Check "Enable Installment Payment"
   - Installment form appears inline

3. **Setup Installments**
   ```
   Installment #1: ₱2,000 - Due: May 1, 2026
   Installment #2: ₱1,500 - Due: June 1, 2026
   Installment #3: ₱1,500 - Due: July 1, 2026
   ```

4. **Place Order**
   - Order created with installment plan
   - Customer receives order
   - Payments tracked in Payment Management
