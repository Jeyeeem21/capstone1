================================================================================
                    KJP RICE MILL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
                     SYSTEM OBJECTIVES (CAPSTONE)
================================================================================


GENERAL OBJECTIVE:
    To develop a web-based rice mill management system for KJP Rice Mill
    that automates and streamlines the end-to-end rice supply chain — from
    procurement of raw palay to product distribution and delivery — while
    providing a public-facing e-commerce website for advertising and selling
    KJP products.


SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES:

1.  To create a system that manages the importation and exportation
    of KJP rice products.

2.  To advertise and showcase KJP rice products through a
    public-facing website.

3.  To manage the procurement of raw palay from multiple suppliers,
    tracking quantity, cost, and transaction details.

4.  To organize procurements into batches by rice variety for
    efficient tracking and bulk processing.

5.  To manage and monitor the drying process of procured palay,
    tracking duration, cost, and drying status.

6.  To manage the rice milling (processing) stage, recording input
    and output quantities and automatically computing yield.

7.  To manage rice product inventory with automated stock computation
    from completed processing output.

8.  To monitor stock levels and provide low-stock and out-of-stock
    alerts for better inventory visibility.

9.  To manage rice product varieties and ensure variety traceability
    throughout the entire supply chain.

10. To manage supplier and customer records with validated contact
    information.

11. To provide an administrative dashboard with key performance
    indicators, trend charts, and operational summaries.

12. To provide a client portal where customers can browse products,
    manage a shopping cart, and place orders online.

13. To provide a driver portal for delivery personnel to manage
    assigned deliveries and update delivery statuses.

14. To provide a staff portal with sales monitoring and inventory
    alerts for day-to-day operations.

15. To manage delivery assignments, track delivery items, and
    support proof-of-delivery documentation.

16. To manage staff records including role assignments and
    employment details.

17. To implement role-based access control with separate portals
    for Admin, Staff, Client, and Driver users.

18. To implement a Content Management System (CMS) that allows the
    admin to update public website content without code changes.

19. To provide a customizable appearance and theme system for the
    admin to personalize the look and feel of the system.

20. To provide a business settings module for managing business
    information, logo, hours, and social media links.

21. To provide a sales management module for tracking sales
    transactions, invoices, and payment statuses.

22. To provide an audit trail that logs all system activities
    for accountability and transparency.

23. To implement a database backup and export feature for data
    security and recovery.

24. To implement a responsive web design that adapts seamlessly
    across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

25. To implement data visualization through interactive charts
    and graphs across all dashboards for data-driven decision
    making.

26. To implement a Point of Sale (POS) module for processing
    walk-in customer transactions.
